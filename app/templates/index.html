Introduction

This can be an about section or anything else ;)



Web research, data science, and artificial intelligence have been rapidly changing our life and society. Researchers and practitioners in the fields take a large amount of time to read literature and compare existing approaches. Tablepedia aims to improve researchers efficiency by automatically reading PDF tables and managing experimental evidences. Tablepedia offers three functions to facilitate research and practice:

(1) finding related methods and datasets

(2) finding top-performing baseline methods

(3) finding conflicting reported numbers. Besides, user could upload their own PDFs to enrich the existing database and use it

Use Tablepedia based on your own experimental results in PDF papers
